Introducing Tor Run

With its clean-lined aesthetic, the Tor Run is the perfect sneaker alternative. Inspired by our heritage, this premium sports silhouette uses crafted seams and colour detailing to create the ideal mix of style and comfort.

  • Shop Womens Tor Run
  • Shop Mens Tor Run

Constructed from a tactile mix of suede, nubuck and full grain leather, the Tor Run’s upper provides softness and unbeatable comfort, while its stitch down construction recalls our iconic design DNA.

A 100% vegetal leather heel and EVA inserts in the forefoot and bottom deliver lightweight flexibility and stability. Because form is nothing without function.

With its Forest Stewardship Council ® 100% certified soles natural rubber crepe sole, the Tor Run features one of our the most socially and environmentally responsible outsoles to date. Made from the sap of the rubber tree - a natural, renewable resource - the FSC label means that the rubber has been harvested with respect for communities, wildlife, and the environment.

Waterproof

Comfort in any weather. With technology that keeps feet dry and comfortable, our shoes have been designed to endure everything the elements can throw at you.

Our GORE-TEX® products are layered with a GORE-TEX® membrane. Pores allow water vapour to pass through, but they’re small enough to prevent water drops from coming in.
